Human Digestive System

The human digestive system breaks down food so the body can absorb nutrients and get rid of waste. Move your hero through the organs to complete the mission list.

  • 👄 Mouth – Chews food and mixes it with saliva to begin digestion.
  • 🧵 Esophagus – Pushes food down to the stomach using muscle contractions.
  • 🍔 Stomach – Mixes food with acid and enzymes to turn it into chyme.
  • 🧬 Small Intestine – Absorbs nutrients into the blood through tiny villi.
  • 🚽 Large Intestine – Absorbs water and forms waste before elimination.
  • 🟤 Liver – Produces bile, which helps digest fats.
  • 🟡 Gallbladder – Stores and releases bile into the small intestine.
  • 🟣 Pancreas – Releases digestive enzymes and controls blood sugar.

Use the arrow keys to explore each organ and read the short description in the info box.
